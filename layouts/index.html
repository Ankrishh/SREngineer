5 Pillars of Site Reliability Engineering (Google SRE)

Share Ownership

Own production with developers and have the same view and approach to production with same tooling.

SLOs & Blameless Postmortems

Have Error Budgets & Service Level Objectives (SLOs) to allow safe innovation. When failures happen, take steps to ensure the failure doesn't repeat in the same way more than once.

Reduce Cost of Failure

Introduce change to small set of users before wider deployment.

Automate this year's job away

Automation first approach to reduce toil and make space for innovation.

Measure Toil & Reliability

Measure Toil. Measure Reliability. Make Progress.
